About Me


I am a nineteen-year-old actress who has been honing my skills since age nine when I was cast in my school’s production of Aladdin, my favorite Disney movie. As a young actress, I've been trained in film-acting by Cheryl Felicia Rhoads and the New York Film Academy. I also have experience acting in several independent projects and student films. In 2018 I was cast in a Super Bowl commercial for Centra Health Hospital. As a dynamic, enthusiastic actress with an ability to work well with everyone, I will make a great addition to any cast!

Height: 5’4”
Eye color: Blue
